Autumn season starts before the starting of winters usually in the month of September. Specifically, the autumn season ranges between September and November in the northern hemisphere. This season is also known as fall season in many countries. Autumn season commences with its own beauty and brings the moments of joy and happiness. This season adds extraordinary beauty in nature and the environment as compared to other seasons. During autumn or fall, you can see the beautiful colours on the tree leaves like vivid yellow, rich reddish-brown, ochre and the scraps of the trees and plants beautifully coloured and splattered around everywhere.
